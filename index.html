About Me

I'm inspiring a lifelong
love of music

I'm a passionate piano teacher based in Entebbe, dedicated to making music education accessible, enjoyable, and transformative for students of every age and background.

My approach blends strong technical foundations with genuine creative freedom — so my students don't just learn to play notes, they learn to feel music.

Patient
Every student learns at their own pace, with no pressure.
Supportive
I create a warm environment where mistakes are part of the journey.
Personalised
I tailor lessons to your goals, interests, and schedule.
Results-Driven
I track your progress so you always know you're growing.

Music is not what I do. It is how I help people discover who they are.

Lessons for All Ages

Find your perfect fit

🧒
Children

Build a strong foundation in a fun, encouraging environment. Young learners develop coordination, discipline, and a lifelong love for music.

  • Note reading & basic theory
  • Finger coordination & posture
  • Simple songs & melodies
  • Confidence through performance
🎓
Teens

Develop real skills, confidence, and a deeper relationship with music. Perfect for beginners or students who want to level up.

  • Technique & musicianship
  • Repertoire building
  • Theory & composition basics
  • Performance preparation
🎹
Adults

It's never too late to start. Whether you're a complete beginner or returning after years away, lessons are shaped entirely around you.

  • Learn songs you actually love
  • Stress-free, judgment-free pace
  • Improvisation & creativity
  • Music reading or playing by ear
